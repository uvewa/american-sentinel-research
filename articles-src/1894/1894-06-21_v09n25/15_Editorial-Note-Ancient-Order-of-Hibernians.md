AN ITEM in the Pilot of the 9th inst., says: "Everywhere throughout the entire country the most gratifying reports reach us as to the growth and progress of the A. O. H." This means simply that the Ancient Order of Hibernians, a Roman Catholic order, armed and drilled under the name of Hibernian Rifles, is growing rapidly in the United States.
