Dennis Ryan, the papist postmaster at Footville, Wis., who refused to let copies of Mr. Linton's speech be circulated to those to whom they were addressed at that place, has signified his intention of sticking to the position taken by him no matter who gives orders to the contrary. Denver American, Aug. 25.
