A LIMITED number of volumes of the AMERICAN SENTINEL for 1893 have been bound and are now ready. Orders will be filled as received. If you want a volume please to order at once. The prices are the same as last year, namely, $1.50 for manila covers, and $2.50 for cloth with leather backs and corners.
