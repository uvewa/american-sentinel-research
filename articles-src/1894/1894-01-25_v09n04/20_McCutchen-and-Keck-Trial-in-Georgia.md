In about a month from now Elder W. A. McCutchen and Professor E. C. Keck, a Seventh-day Adventist minister and a Seventh-day Adventist teacher, will be placed on trial in Gainesville, Ga., for Sunday work. If convicted, they can be punished by fine in any sum not to exceed $1,000, imprisonment not to exceed six months, or work in the chain-gang not to exceed twelve months. *Any one or more of these punishments may be imposed at the discretion of the court.*
