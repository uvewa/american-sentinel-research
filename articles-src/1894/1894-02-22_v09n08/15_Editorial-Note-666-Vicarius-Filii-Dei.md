A FOOT note on Rev. 13:18, in the Roman Catholic version of the Scriptures, says: "Six hundred sixty-six. The numeral letters of his name shall make up this number." The pope styles himself, "The Vicar of the Son of God," which title he wears in Latin upon his miter: "Vicarius Filii Dei." The numerical value of these letters is exactly 666; u being formerly the same as v.
