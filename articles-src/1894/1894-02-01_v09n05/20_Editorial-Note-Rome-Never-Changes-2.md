"ROME never changes," hence Rome of the Spanish Inquisition and of the massacre of St Bartholomew, and of Smithfield, is the same Rome which to-day fawns upon and flatters the American Republic while she surely and swiftly casts her chains about its free institutions that she may, erelong, drag it at her chariot wheels.
