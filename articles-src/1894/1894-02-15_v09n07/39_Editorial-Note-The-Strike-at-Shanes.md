"The Strike at Shane's," is a good book for those to read who have anything to do with animals. It is the sequel to "Black Beauty," and is published by the American Humane Society, 19 Milk St., Boston. Ninety-two pages. Sent by mail on receipt of ten cents. Address the publishers.
