THE SEVENTH-DAY ADVENTIST CHURCH BUILDING AT CHURCH HILL, MD., HAS BEEN DEDICATED, NOTWITHSTANDING THE THREATS OF A NUMBER OF RUDE FELLOWS OF THE BASKET SORT, WHO SUPPOSED THAT THEY COULD FIGHTEN AN ADVENTIST PREACHER BY WHITECAB METHODS.
