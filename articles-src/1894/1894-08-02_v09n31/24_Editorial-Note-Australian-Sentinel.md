THE FIRST NUMBER OF THE AUSTRALIAN SENTINEL AND HERALD OF LIBERTY, PUBLISHED IN MELBOURNE, HAS REACHED OUR TABLE. THE AUSTRALIAN SENTINEL, "SET FOR THE DEFENSE OF LIBERTY OF CONSCIENCE, AND IS THEREFORE UNCOMPROMISINGLY OPPOSED TO A UNION OF CHURCH AND STATE, EITHER IN NAME OR IN FACT." ITS EDITORS ARE NOT UNKNOWN TO OUR READERS, BEING A. G. DANIELS, W. C. COLORED AND J. O. CORLISS. WE WISH THIS NEW ADVOCATE OF SOUL LIBERTY PROSPECTLY IN ITS GOOD WORK.
