IT IS STATED THAT "THE GIVING OF THE BALIOT TO THE WOMEN OF COLORADO IS IMPARTING NEW LIFE TO THE WOMAN'S CHRISTIAN TEMPERANCE UNION OF THAT STATE. IT HAS PLACED THEM IN A POSITION WHERE EVERY BLOW COUNTS, AND THEY ARE VIGOROUSLY IMPROVING THEIR OPPORTUNITY." NOT A BIT OF DOUBT OF IT!
