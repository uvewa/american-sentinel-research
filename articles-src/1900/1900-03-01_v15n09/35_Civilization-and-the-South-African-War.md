It is difficult to sift the truth from the mass of conflicting reports that come from the battle fields in South Africa; but one thing seems clear, and that is that the civilization of Paris, London, or New York is not essential to the development of those physical and intellectual qualities which make men heroes. The world would be just as well off to-day if it were not quite so highly civilized.
