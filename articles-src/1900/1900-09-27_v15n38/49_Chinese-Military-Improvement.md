From the time of China's war with Japan to that of the "Boxer" uprising the Chinese have evidently been making rapid improvement in the art of fighting with modern weapons, and if they continue this rate of improvement the European Powers may ere long find that in essaying to "civilize" China they have undertaken a larger contract than they can execute.
