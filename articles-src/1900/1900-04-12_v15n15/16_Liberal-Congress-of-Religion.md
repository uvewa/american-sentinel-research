A "LIBERAL congress of religion," in furtherance of the movement inaugurated by these congresses some years ago, will be convened in Boston April 24, continuing to the 29th. Its purpose as announced "is to unite in fraternal conference men and women of whatever name, who believe in the application of religious principles and spiritual forces to the present problems of life."
