A recent telegram from London tells of the Bubonic plague at Mauritius, Bombay, Calcutta, Port Said, Teheran, Manila, Hong Kong, Melbourne, Sydney, Noume, Brisbane, Perth and Aukland; also in Buenos Ayres, where the disease has been raging since the middle of March. To this list should be added Honolulu.
