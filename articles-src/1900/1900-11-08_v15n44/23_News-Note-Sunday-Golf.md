SUNDAY golf is said to be causing much discussion among golf players themselves at the present time in New England. The *Defender* suggests that "some timely, forceful sermons on 'amusements, their legitimate place, and the time for enjoying them,' delivered from pulpits, might have a re-straining and healthful influence just now, if ministers would only preach them."
