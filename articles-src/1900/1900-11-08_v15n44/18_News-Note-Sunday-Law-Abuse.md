"We shall be pleased," says the organ of the "New England Sabbath Protective League," "to come into contact with all friends of the Lord's day in New England who believe that there are abuses which can be and should be remedied." We are not in New England, but we can point out one abuse that should be remedied; namely, the one very common in many places of invoking the civil law to compel all the people to keep Sunday simply because some of the people believe that keeping that day is a religious duty.
