THE Baltimore Sun says of the Sanford bill for modifying the present Sunday law in Baltimore, that "If the question were put to a popular vote the Blue Sunday-ites would be bluer than ever when the vote was counted, for they would be beaten by the most overwhelming majority ever given in this city.
