The leading nations of Europe have come to an agreement to prevent the further killing of big game in Africa, except with their mutual consent; but meanwhile the killing of the human species—men, women and children—goes on as fast as the combined efforts of two opposing powers can continue the process. It is too much to expect that European civilization should exert its force to prevent the further slaughter of human beings by the bullets of men who hunt beneath the government flag.
