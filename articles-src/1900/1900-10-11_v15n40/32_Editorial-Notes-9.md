Any one receiving The Sentinel of Liberty without having ordered it, may know that it is sent by some friend. Those who have not ordered The Sentinel need have no fears that they will be asked to pay for it.

## EXAMINE THE LABEL.

The yellow label on your paper indicates the date on which your subscription expires. Please examine it closely, and if it reads 19 you should forward your renewal without delay in order to avoid the loss of any papers.
