Apropos of the Indian famine and of the utter inadequacy of the relief measures, the Springfield Republican says: "What a melancholy reflection it is that so many millions of pounds sterling and American dollars should be expended on unrighteous war; instead of in relief of the greatest human distress by starvation and cholera."
