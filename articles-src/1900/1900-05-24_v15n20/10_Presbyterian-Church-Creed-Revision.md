The General Assembly of the Presbyterian Church convened in St. Louis on the 17th inst. The paramount question to be considered is the revision of the Westminster Confession of Faith. Amending a creed is not an easy task and it is by no means certain that the advocates of revision will be able to muster sufficient votes to make the desired change.
