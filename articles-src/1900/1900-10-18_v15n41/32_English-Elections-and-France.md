THE result of the recent elections in England has alarmed France. The emphatic endorsement of the South African war by the voters is regarded as a personal triumph for Joseph Chamberlain, and the French fear that this means that "an era of jingoism will be inaugurated which will seriously threaten the peace of Europe."
