CONSIDERABLE alarm continues to be expressed because of the triumph in the late elections in Cuba of the Nationalists, who are stigmatized even by such papers as the *Independent* as "revolutionists." And why "revolutionists"? Simply because they demand absolute independence of the United States. It is conceded, however, that a majority of the delegates to the constitutional convention are men of ability.
