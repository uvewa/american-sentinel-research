"MY MOTHER'S LIFE," by Mary Henry Rossiter, with an introduction by Bishop John H. Vincent of the Methodist Episcopal Church, is a most interesting sketch of the life and work of Mrs. S. M. I. Henry by her daughter. The book contains over three hundred and fifty pages, is handsomely bound in cloth, and is published by Fleming H. Revell Company of this city. It is a most helpful book well worth reading. Price, $1.50. For particulars address the publishers.
