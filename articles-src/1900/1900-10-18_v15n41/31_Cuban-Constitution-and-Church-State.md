SPEAKING of the new constitution to be framed for Cuba the Havana correspondent of the Catholic Mirror says: "There is little doubt but that every draft of a constitution agreed upon by the various parties will provide for freedom of worship and an entire separation of church and state. Herein the Cubans will build wisely."
