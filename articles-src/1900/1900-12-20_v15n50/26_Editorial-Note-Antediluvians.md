Do you think we are making a big fuss about nothing in what we are saying about threatening perils in this Republic? Well, that is just what the antediluvians thought about the things said to them by Noah. Remember that so great an event as the destruction of the world once came upon the people without their perceiving its coming till they found themselves actually involved in it.
