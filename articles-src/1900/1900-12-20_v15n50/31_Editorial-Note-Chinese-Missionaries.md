"Those missionaries," says the Monitor (Roman Catholic), "who are represented as demanding and insisting upon the wholesale decapitation of prominent Chinese officials 'in the interest of Christianity in China,' are supplying the celestials with a very potent argument against future toleration of foreign missionaries."
