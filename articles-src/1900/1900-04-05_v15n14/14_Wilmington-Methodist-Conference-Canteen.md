The Wilmington Methodist Episcopal conference at Wilmington, Del., March 27, adopted a resolution condemning the army canteen and the use and sale of liquor in new possessions of this Government; also a resolution requesting the general conference to censure President McKinley for setting aside the anti-canteen law.
