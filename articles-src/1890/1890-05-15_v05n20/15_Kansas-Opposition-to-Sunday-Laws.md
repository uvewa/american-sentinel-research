DISTRICT SECRETARY C. Mc REYNOLDS, of Kansas, writes a very encouraging letter from Winfield, in his State. National Reform and Sabbath Union workers have been very active there the past few weeks, but their efforts have developed able and intelligent opposition, in some very unexpected quarters.
