OUR esteemed Republican contemporary, the *Tribune,* reports that the experiment in State socialism which the State of New York entered upon when it determined to pay the laborers it employs higher wages than the market rate, is to be continued, and that the Legislature will have to appropriate $600,000 to bear the expense of keeping it up during 1890.
