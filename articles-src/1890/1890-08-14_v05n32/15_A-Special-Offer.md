ANY one—either new or old subscriber—mentioning this notice, and sending us one dollar for one year's subscription to THE AMERICAN SENTINEL within 30 days from the date of this paper, will receive by return mail, post-paid, a copy of either "Civil Government and Religion," or "The National Sunday Law," both of which are advertised on the preceding page.
