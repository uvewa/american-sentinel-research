THE *Southern Sentinel,* a Dallas, Texas, paper, that has for some months been doing good service in the promulgation of correct political doctrines in the Lone Star State, has just been enlarged. The principles of the *Southern Sentinel* are substantially the same as our own, and we rejoice in its prosperity.
