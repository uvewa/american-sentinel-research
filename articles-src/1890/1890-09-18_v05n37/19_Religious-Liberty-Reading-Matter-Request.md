MR. B. L. DIFFENBACHER, 1201 Market Street, Sioux City, Iowa, wishes Religious Liberty reading matter for free distribution during the Corn Palace Carnival, in that city, September 25, to October 11. Unsoiled copies of the AMERICAN SENTINEL or numbers of the "Sentinel Library," will be thankfully received, and judiciously used. Send by mail or express, prepaid, to the above address.
