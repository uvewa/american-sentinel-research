OON National Reform exchanges, that want Sunday laws, "but without oppressing any who conscientiously observe the seventh day," are as silent as the grave in regard to the persecution of Seventh-day Adventists in Tennessee. What do the editors of the Christian Statesman think about sending honest, God-fearing men to jail upon the strength of the legal fiction that they were "guilty of a public nuisance by working on Sunday, to the prejudice of society, and against the peace and dignity of the State"?
