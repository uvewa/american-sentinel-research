THE platform of the People's Party, of Polk County, Minnesota, adopted by the Convention held at Crookston, May 24, 1892, contains this paragraph:

"That we favor the opening of the World's Columbian Exposition every day of the week."

The Convention, which adopted the platform, consisted of one hundred delegates representing forty-five hundred voters.
