THE RUSSIAN GOVERNMENT seems to hesitate to go to absolute extremes in its warfare on the Protestants of the Baltic provinces. In the matter of reducing the theological school at Dorpat to a mere academy, or of transferring it to some smaller town, the ministry committee has advised delay.
