These sturdy-armed, stout-hearted settlers gave to these new-born communities their character and started our young nation on its course as a Christian country.

The good-natured claim of the Puritans is often misquoted and unsubstantiated the country's best days by trading purely on spiritual power, which political and religious force from the word was established.
