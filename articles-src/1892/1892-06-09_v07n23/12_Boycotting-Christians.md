The complaint that the conscience of Christian people all over the land is being shocked because Sunday opening agents have thrown open the Fair grounds on that day is not in accordance with the facts. This is letting the camel's head in with a vengeance. The boycott of the whole Christian people of America involves forcing the rest of the Fair to so far conform to American ecclesiastical requirements, no matter what the voluntary regrets the day or manner.
