At the Illinois State Prohibition Convention lately held at Springfield, Ill., a resolution was adopted declaring in favor of closing the World's Fair on Sunday. These Prohibitionists are looking forward to fusion with the National Reformers, the American Sabbath Unionists, and all their following, through which to hold the balance of political power and control legislation, State and national.
