On June 7, Mr. Layton, of Ohio, presented in the House of Representatives, "Resolutions of District Grand Lodge, No. 3, of the Independent Order of B'nai B'rith, Ohio, protesting against the action of any and all governments, having treaty relations with the United States, whereby any of our citizens are discriminated against because of their religious faith."
