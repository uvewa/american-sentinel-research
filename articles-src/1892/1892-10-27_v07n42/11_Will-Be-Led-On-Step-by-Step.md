All religious despotism begins by combination of religious bodies, and the movement to impose upon the political institutions of a country, and the peculiar doctrines and customs of the Church. That is what the "combination" of religious bodies for political purposes, we see in the country in our recent Congress, of which our lobbying clergymen and their political managers have grown in their hand. The civil power has been under the hands of the religious, the persecution, the awful ruin, and yet the triumph of the Lord is the end just as surely.
