# Degradation of Sacred Things.

It has long been a subject of remark that many people mix up religion and politics in a way which seems strange to us in this country. Not a few preachers and editors of religious journals have boldly and distinctly done so. A leading New York pastor has recently published an astonishing plan for this sort of thing in which he says:

A genuine can not be degrading the dignity of religion by taking advantage of a presidential campaign encouragement for the announcement of great religious truths, and the advocacy of Christian principles.

If we put the spirit of Christ rightly into the hearts of both buyers and selling? all

As if this were not enough he closes himself with heated argument with his own text.
