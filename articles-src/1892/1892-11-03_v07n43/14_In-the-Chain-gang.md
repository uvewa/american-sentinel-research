HEAVEN, have you done anything to be in a crusading trail, in the end of this? "In the Chaining for Conscience" is published in a large pamphlet 15 pages? If not, be sure to send at once to the National Religious Liberty Association, Battle Creek, Mich., for a good supply and then scatter them among your friends and neighbors.

Also order copies of the pamphlet, "The persecution of Seventh-day Adventists" it is the first hundred, and it will surely be read by one hundred copies for $1.00 per hundred.
