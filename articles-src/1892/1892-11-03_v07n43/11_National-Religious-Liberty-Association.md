That eminent moral philosopher and Christian, John Locke, nevertheless, thus sets forth its principles in defending the perpetuity of the marriage relation.

The end of separation between male and female being not barely procreation, but the continuation of the species, the conjunction betwixt man and wife ought to last, even after procreation, so long as is necessary for the nourishment of the young ones, who are to be sustained by those that got them, till they are able to shift and provide for themselves.
