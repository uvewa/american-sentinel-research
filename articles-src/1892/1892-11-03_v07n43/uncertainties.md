# Uncertainties - American Sentinel v07n43, November 3, 1892

## General Notes

This issue presented significant OCR challenges due to:
- Dense text in small type across 3-column layout
- Some areas of reduced scan quality
- Overlapping text in some areas of pages 1-2

## Specific Uncertainties

### Pages 1-2, Lead Editorial "The Record of the Proceedings of Congress"
- **Second pass completed:** Yes
- **Issue:** The Congressional Record quotations contain specific page numbers and procedural language that was difficult to read precisely in some areas.
- **Confidence:** MEDIUM - The main arguments and quotations are clear, but some of the procedural details and page references may have minor errors.
- **Transcribed text:** "the Lord blessed Saturday day, and hallowed it" - this appears to be an unusual reading where "Saturday" may be a deliberate editorial choice or misprint in the original.
