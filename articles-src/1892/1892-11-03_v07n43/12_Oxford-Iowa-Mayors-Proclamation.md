Oxford, Iowa, Oct. 6, 1892.

To the business men of Oxford:

You are hereby requested to keep your public places of business closed on the Sabbath days, on and after the publication of this notice, or suffer the penalties of the law and ordinance of the incorporated town of Oxford.

A. J. DARNER, Mayor.
