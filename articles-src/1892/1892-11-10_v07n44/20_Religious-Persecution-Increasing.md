The *Western Herald* (Burlington, Iowa), commenting on the recent arrest of some Jewish stone cutters, at Dubuque, remarks that "religious persecutions seem to be on the increase all over the country, notwithstanding the constitutional guarantees of the rights of conscience and religious liberty."
