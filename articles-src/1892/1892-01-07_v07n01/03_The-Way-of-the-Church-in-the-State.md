It is pitiable that Mr. Tinker does not know that the Church in this country "presumes to dictate in secular and governmental matters" as much as it can, or that "she assumes the support of a martialist, is patronized and fed by the bounties of the State" to the extent of millions of dollars in the form of exemption from taxation! it is not possible. The very strength of which he has in his parlor the recipient of State bounty to the extent perhaps of one or two thousand dollars a year and he must know it. How, then, does he not interpret the foregoing correctly? Is Mr. Tinker a fraud? Or does he take himself to be so much a part of the very strength of the Church that can not be known until it ceases to be legally favored by the State?
