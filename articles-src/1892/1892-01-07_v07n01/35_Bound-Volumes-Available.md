THOSE who wish bound volumes of THE AMERICAN SENTINEL, for 1891, should send their names at once. Only a limited number will be bound, and orders will be filled as they are received. The prices will be the same as last year, namely, heavy manilla paper, $1.50, half roan, (cloth, with leather back) $2.50.
