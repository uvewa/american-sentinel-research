On June 30, Mr. Durborow, Chairman of the House Committee on the World's Fair, presented in the House of Representatives petitions from Edward Everett Hale, and seventy-nine others, Unitarian ministers of America; and from the American Unitarian Association of Boston, Mass., and the Western Unitarian Conference of Chicago, as organizations; also from Jenkin Lloyd Jones, Emil G. Hirsch, LL. D., and twenty-six other liberal ministers of the West, all asking that Congress should refrain from any legislation respecting Sunday at the World's Fair.
