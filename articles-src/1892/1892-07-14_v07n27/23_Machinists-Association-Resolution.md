The International Convention of the Machinist's Association of North America, in a late meeting at Chicago, at which two hundred and twenty delegates were present, from the United States, Canada, and Mexico, representing labor organizations from these different sections, passed resolutions asking that the World's Fair be kept open on Sundays.
