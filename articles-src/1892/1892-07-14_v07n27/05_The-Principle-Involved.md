BUT IT WAS NOT THE AMOUNT OF THE FINE AND COSTS THAT CAUSED THESE MEN TO GO TO JAIL; IT WAS THE PRINCIPLE INVOLVED. NEITHER WAS IT, AS THE STATESMAN WICKEDLY ASSERTS, TO AWAKEN SYMPATHY. THEY could not conscientiously pay fines imposed upon them for exercising a God-given right. This fact had been previously published in THE SENTINEL so that the Statesman is without excuse in making the statement that it does.
