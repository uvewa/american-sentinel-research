Church and State, Are They to Be United?

HAD every American citizen understood the history of his country as well as we have done, we need never have had the war between the nations that is and then lastly of all wars.

Did every American citizen understand (as he should) did the danger of any enactment in regard to any religion or religious observance whatever, the union of Church and State, that worst of all despotisms, would never take place in this free country.
