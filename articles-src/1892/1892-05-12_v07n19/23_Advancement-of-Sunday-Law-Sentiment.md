THE Secretary of the National Religious Liberty Association writes, "From all parts of the country we are receiving communications every day showing the rapid advancement of sentiment toward universal Sunday laws. The general offices of the Religious Liberty Association are being crowded to their utmost capacity in their endeavors to meet demands upon them."
