THE State Sabbath Association of Illinois, at its annual meeting just held, passed resolutions especially requesting the State of Illinois to close its individual State exhibit at the coming World's Fair, on Sunday, irrespective of what might be the decision of the Commissioners as to the Exposition as a whole.
