CONGRESSMAN CAMPBELL, of this city, on the 6th inst., introduced a bill into Congress which provides for the cancellation of all taxes and assessment of any kind or nature, now or heretofore levied by the District upon the building and ground actually occupied and used by the House of the Good Shepherd of the City of Washington.
