WE are requested to say that Mr. E. M. Macdonald, who has since the death of D. M. Bennett been the editor of the Truth Seeker, the New York Freethought journal, has become the business as well as the editorial manager. Persons having relations with the Truth Seeker should address him at 28 Lafayette place, New York City.
