This daily paper contains a dispatch in regard to a United States Senator from a Western State, who has resigned his position as school commissioner, under reasons relating the facts. The school commissioner, in this that he does not need a civil law to compel everybody to rest on the day he wants to keep, in order that he may observe it himself. Would not the same truth hold good in regard to those who really desire to keep Sunday? If a man has a resting place in his own home, he will rest if he has the day, and a situation or job of work will not long disturb him.
