THE *Mail and Express* is delighted with the Georgia decision that a man must not be honest, that he need not pay bills contracted for Sunday service, that it says:—

In New York State, unfortunately, no such laws exist, and the immorality of unrestricted Sunday papers may amount to a continuous and standing yearly outrage.

Very "unfortunately" indeed — for sigpiracy and intolerance! Is the *Mail and Express* troubled by the competition of the Sunday papers of New York?
