This is not written in defense of those outrages, for the writer has the most hearty sympathy for those courageous missionaries who have taken their lives in their hands to carry the glad tidings to the benighted. And we hold they deserve just as much respect, and no more, for the purpose as any of the citizens of the United States of America.
