## NATIONAL Religious Liberty Association.

### DECLARATION OF PRINCIPLES.

We believe in the religion taught by Jesus Christ. We believe in temperance, and regard for the highest interests of man. We also believe it is the right of every individual to worship or not worship according to the dictates of his own conscience. We also believe it is the right of and not of any government to legislate on matters of religion, and therefore we are opposed to all religious legislation.

A. F. B.
