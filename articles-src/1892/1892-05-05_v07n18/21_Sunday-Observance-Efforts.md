AT Nashville, Tennessee, Warren, Pennsylvania, and Findlay, Ohio, efforts have lately been made for compulsory Sunday observance.

FORT WAYNE, Indiana, has organized a Best-day League and appointed its leading ministers as officers. The avowed intention is here, as elsewhere to secure the enforcement of Sunday laws.
