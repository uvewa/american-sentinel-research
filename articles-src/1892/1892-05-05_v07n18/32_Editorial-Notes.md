THE Mail and Express thinks that "Michael Walsh, Ph.D., is in a position of more usefulness as treasurer of the ministerial fund for the Grant monument than as editor of a Sunday paper." The Mail and Express is an evening paper, and evening papers do not publish Sunday editions. It would not pay.
