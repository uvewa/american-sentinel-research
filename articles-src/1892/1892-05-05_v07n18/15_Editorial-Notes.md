FRIENDS sending us newspaper clippings are requested to invariably note on the clippings themselves the name and date of the paper, also where published. Clippings of this little without this information.

---

Mr. Akin and Rev. Express thinks that "Ministerial Wash, Ph.D., is in a position of more announce as a treasure of the ministered field for the Grant Foundation than as editor of a newspaper. The *Mail and Express* is an evening paper, and evening papers do not publish Sunday editions.

---

SEVERAL Russian Hebrew boys, all under sixteen years of age, were arrested in Central Park, on a recent Sunday, for sailing boats. They spent the night in cells, and were arraigned in a police court of New York the next day. One of the boys, Louis Severachomas, thirteen years old, spoke for the crowd. He said he was the oldest of seven children. He had attended school every year. The boys were all discharged with a caution not to again offend against the majesty of the law and the sacredness of Sunday.

RELIGIOUS persecution and intolerance of the worst is the world. They are without contradiction or pity.---A. D. Fed.
