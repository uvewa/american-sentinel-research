# Sunday in Our Large Cities.

WHAT the American Sabbath Union and their allies are planning to bring every body indoors on Sundays, or else compel them to go to church, seems, more liberal, and devoting means to obtain the children, men and women, and the sickly, pitched, and pale children, to get a breath of fresh air and exercise. Their plans are practicable we do not pretend to say; but they do show the utter heartlessness of those who would deprive the tired workers of their weekly outing during the summer months.
