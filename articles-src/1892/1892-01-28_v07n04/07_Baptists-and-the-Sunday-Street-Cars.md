In the discussion in Toronto, Ontario, over running the street cars on Sunday, the Baptist ministers of that city refused dissented from the ministers of the other denominations. As a well-informed correspondent of the *Christian at Work* says about this: the Baptists are not Torries when the proposition was to run Toronto’s cars on Sunday. The city council recently said to the people that if they would secure a given number of names to a petition, praying that the cases be run on Sunday, they would submit the question to be the vote of the people. To this action of the council the Methodist ministers issued a resolution, saying that the question was not one to be decided by the people.
