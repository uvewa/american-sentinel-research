# Higher Spontaneity.

> "AND I, John, behold another star come up out of the earth; and he had two horns like a lamb." Rev. 13:11.

For many years Seventh-day Adventists have been keeping their eyes upon the United States of America, holding the firm view that the United States from this point of view holds a place in prophecy; and those who are striving to walk consistent therewith, are not deceived at the end, spiritual powers on earth in by-gone days. Of late, it becomes more and more manifest that the spirit of intolerance and opposition existing and was growing in this Government, but within the last week an event has taken place which is of tremendous significance in connection with the fulfilment of this destiny of the nation. The United States Senate and the members of the United States have united in saying by Congressional enactment, that the World's Columbian Exposition shall have its gates closed against all visitors on Sabbath. They have declared, speaking with the voice of the Nation, that here in this hitherto free land a religion of no nation shall be enforced by law; for legislation always means compelling.
