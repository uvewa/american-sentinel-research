# Flippancy.

HARMLESS, impersonal satire is above ugly hypocrisy; expected; but flippancy of ideas, or meaning at the opinions of other people upon important religious subjects, places the sincere in the seat of the scorner, and violates the express command of Jesus Christ (see Matt. 5:33).
