WIFE—I'd like to know what you go to the club for on Sundays. Are the card rooms and billiard rooms open on Sundays?

Husband—Oh, no, my dear—no, indeed. We have a study of each and meditation. Everything is closed but the bar.—*New York Bar.*

Fred Brody.
