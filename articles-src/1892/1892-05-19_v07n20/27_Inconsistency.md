INCONSISTENCY can scarcely go farther than it does when those who advocate the Sunday-closing of the Chicago Exposition, say, in one breath, that Christians in general are increasingly lax and inconsistent in their personal habits concerning Sunday-observance, and in the next, that the opening of the Exhibition on Sunday "will outrage the religious sentiment of millions of people."
