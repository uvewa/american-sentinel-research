New York "World," January 25.

There will be a meeting of the Executive Committee of St. Michael's Chapter of the Church Association for the Advancement of the Interests of Labor to-morrow night at the Parish House of St. Michael's Church, 225 West Ninety-ninth Street [New York City]. The object is to start a movement to close drug stores on Sundays. So far sixty-five trade, labor, reform, business, and church organizations and fifteen leading clergymen have indorsed the movement.
