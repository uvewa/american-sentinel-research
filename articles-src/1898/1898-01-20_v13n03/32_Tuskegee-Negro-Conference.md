THE usual annual session of the Tuskegee Negro Conference will take place in Tuskegee, Ala., Wednesday, February 23rd. The Workers' Conference, composed of officers and teachers of the various colored schools in the South, takes place February 24th, at Tuskegee, Ala. These conferences present an opportunity to study the condition and progress of the negro that is afforded nowhere else. Further information can be had from Booker T. Washington, Tuskegee, Ala.
