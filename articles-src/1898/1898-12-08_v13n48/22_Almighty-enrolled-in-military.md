One would almost think, from reading what is said by "patriotic" clergymen and others in reference to the late war, that the Almighty is enrolled in the military and naval forces of the nation. And all civilized nations appear to be equally sure that the Almighty is on their side. But the Omnipotent never joins any side. He only invites all men to join his side.
