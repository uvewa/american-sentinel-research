TO SAY THAT THE SABBATH NEEDS THE SUPPORT OF HUMAN ENACTMENTS, IS TO SAY THAT THE LAW OF GOD NEEDS SUCH SUPPORT; AND THAT IS TO SAY THAT THE LAW OF GOD IS VERY WEAK.
