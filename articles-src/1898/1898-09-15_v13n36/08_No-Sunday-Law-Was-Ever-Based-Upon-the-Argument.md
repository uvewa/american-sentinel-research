NO SUNDAY LAW WAS EVER BASED UPON THE ARGUMENT THAT THE FIRST DAY OF THE WEEK IS THE SEVENTH DAY, OR THAT ONE DAY OF THE WEEK WILL DO A-WELL FOR THE SABBATH AS ANOTHER.
