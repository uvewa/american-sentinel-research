THERE ARE NO PEOPLE WHO ARE MORE MARKED AS HAVING A WEEKLY DAY OF REST THAN ARE OBSERVERS OF THE SEVENTH DAY. YET THEY NEVER HAD A LAW TO "GIVE" THEM THEIR DAY, OR TO "PROTECT" IT, IN ANY MANNER.
