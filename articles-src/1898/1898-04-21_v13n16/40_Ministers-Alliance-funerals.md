THE MINISTERS' ALLIANCE OF KANSAS CITY, MO., HAS ENTERED A PROTEST AGAINST CONDUCTING FUNERALS ON SUNDAY, ON THE GROUND THAT IT IS A DESECRation OF THE SABBATH.
