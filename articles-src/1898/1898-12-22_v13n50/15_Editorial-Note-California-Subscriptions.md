"We are anxious to do all we can for the spread of the truth, although we are very poor, and my husband is an invalid," writes a friend from California, enclosing a list of twelve names, with money to pay for the same, for the SENTINEL, six months to each.

Friend, this is the spirit to pattern after. Are you anxious?
