The timely suggestion is made by the Christian Citizen that as it is likely Spain will kill as many American men before the war is over as the nation can afford to spare, it would be well to take some measures to provide that no more of our boys be killed on the Fourth of July than is absolutely necessary to the proper celebration of the day.
