Hawaii, it is stated, will not become a Territory like those sections of the United States not admitted to the Union as States, but will be a district under military rule, according to the following proclamation lately issued by General Merriam: "For better administration, and subject to the approval of the Secretary of War, the territory lately constituted the Hawaiian republic is hereby constituted a military district, to be known as the District of Hawaii, to be under the command of Brigadier-General Charles King, United States Volunteers, with headquarters at Honolulu."
