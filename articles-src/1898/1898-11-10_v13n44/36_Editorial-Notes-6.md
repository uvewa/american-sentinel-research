A call has been issued for a national "Christian Citizenship" conference, to be held in Washington, December 13â€“15 next. The topics to be discussed are: the limitation of the right of suffrage, civil service reform, national laws forbidding prize-fighting, restricting divorce, and forbidding polygamy; the "American civil Sabbath;" the army canteen; and the prohibition policy as related to Alaska and the Indian Territory. The call is signed by Rev. Josiah Strong, president of the League for Social Service; Anthony Comstock, Secretary of the Society for the Prevention of Vice; Mrs. Mary H. Hunt; and Mrs. M. D. Ellis, of the W. C. T. U., and others.
