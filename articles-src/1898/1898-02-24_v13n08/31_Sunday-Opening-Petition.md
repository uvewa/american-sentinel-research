VICE-PRESIDENT HOBART recently laid before the Senate a petition from the German American Union of Washington, D. C., asking for the opening on Sundays of the Congressional Library, the National Museum, and the Botanic Gardens, between the hours of 1 and 5 P.M. The proposed measure is favored by the leading papers of the city.
