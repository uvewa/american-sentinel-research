From far off Nevada we have just received a list of eleven names,—ten for one year, and one for six months,—accompanied with $10.50, full price for each subscription.

"Please send me a few sample copies. I may send you more soon," is the way he finished his letter.

This, dear reader, reveals the true missionary spirit. Does it touch a chord in your heart? What are you doing? Time is fast passing.
