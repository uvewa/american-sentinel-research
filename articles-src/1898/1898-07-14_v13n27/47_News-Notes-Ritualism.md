RITUALISTIC practices are found in fully half of the churches in England, according to the Tourists' Church Guide, published by the English Church Union. Out of 8,183 churches 4,334 use altar lights, 4,030 the mixed chalice, 2,026 vestments, while in 7,044 the eastward position is observed for the altar.
