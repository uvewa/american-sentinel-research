The casualties of the war with Spain, from the official records of the War Department, as far as completed, were 264 killed in battle. Of these 33 were officers and 231 enlisted men. The figures apply to the losses in the Philippines, Cuba, and Porto Rico. The percentage of officers killed is said to be unprecedented in the battles of the world.
