WAR between England and the Transvaal is once more threatened owing to the failure of recent negotiations between the representative of Great Britain and President Kruger. The negotiations related to a redress of grievances suffered by the "Outlanders"—who are mostly British—in the Transvaal, and their failure is taken to mark the end of diplomacy in relation to the matter.
