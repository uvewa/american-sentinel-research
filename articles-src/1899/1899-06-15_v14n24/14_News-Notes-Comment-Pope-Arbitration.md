CABLE dispatches from Rome state that a vigorous effort is being made there to have the pope appointed head of the International Arbitration commission which is being projected by the Peace Congress at The Hague. If that can be done the pope will doubtless be willing to forgive the slight put upon him in refusing him representation at the congress.
