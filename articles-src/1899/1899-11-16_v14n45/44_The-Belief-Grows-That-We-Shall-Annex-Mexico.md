"The belief grows," said the New York Press the other day, "that we shall annex Mexico." Various facts indicate to that jubilant advocate of destiny "that we shall go on expanding until both continents are ours." Yet whenever some South American state shows signs of uneasiness over the possible extensionist designs of American imperialists, the great organs of imperialism gravely assure it that its apprehensions are groundless.
