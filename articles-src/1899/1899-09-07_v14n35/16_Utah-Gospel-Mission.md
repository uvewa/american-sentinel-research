A movement which combats Mormonism in the proper and only effective way has been started by Rev. John D. Nutting, in the shape of a "Utah Gospel Mission Movement." It is proposed to have traveling missionaries to go from place to place, hold meetings and distribute gospel literature among the Mormons.
