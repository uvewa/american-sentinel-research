We are sometimes accused of tearing down without doing anything to build up. The SENTINEL, however, calls for the preservation of what is already built up,—the Constitution and the principles of freedom established by the founders of the nation—which many are now trying to tear down. We are not tearing down; we are trying to stop the tearing down.
