IF AMERICA IS TO TEACH THE PHILIPPINE NATIVES REPUBLICAN GOVERNMENT, IT MUST TEACH THEM FIRST OF ALL, RESPECT FOR THE PRINCIPLE OF GOVERNMENT BY THE CONSENT OF THE GOVERNED. BUT HOW IS AMERICA PREPARED TO DO THIS WHEN IT HAS ITSELF VIOLATED THIS VERY PRINCIPLE BEFORE THEIR EYES? THE STEP WHICH WAS THOUGHT TO BE NECESSARY TO ESTABLISH GOOD GOVERNMENT AMONG THAT PEOPLE, HAS ONLY PLANTED AN OBSTACLE IN THE WAY OF ITS REALIZATION.
