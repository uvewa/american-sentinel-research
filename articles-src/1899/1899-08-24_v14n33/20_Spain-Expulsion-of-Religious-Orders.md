A DISPATCH from San Sebastian, Spain, says that the republican and socialistic parties have agreed to organize a campaign, demanding the expulsion from Spain of all the religious orders. Considering the extent to which Spain is to-day and has been for centuries dominated by these orders, it is evident that this political combination has undertaken a tremendous task.
