WHAT is the use of trying to overthrow the demon of Sabbath desecration, by means which have proved wholly inadequate to dethrone the demon of intemperance? Only that which will cast out the latter from its seat of power in the heart, can put down the former. The true remedy for one moral evil is a remedy for all.
