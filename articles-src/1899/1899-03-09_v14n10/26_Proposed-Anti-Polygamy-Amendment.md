It is proposed to abolish polygamy in the United States by amendment to the national Constitution. The proposed amendment would be the sixteenth in number, and has been recommended by the House Committee (of Congress) on the Election of President and Vice-President, in the following form:â€”

"SECTION 1. Polygamy shall never exist within the United States or any place subject to their jurisdiction.

"SECTION 2. Congress shall have power to make all needful laws to enforce this article and punish its violation."
