The papacy is gaining ground against the government in Rome. A dispatch from Rome under date of June 25, says: "For the first time since 1870 the Vatican party has obtained a majority in Rome in the municipal elections, and the fronts of the churches are fantastically illuminated nightly in honor of the success."
