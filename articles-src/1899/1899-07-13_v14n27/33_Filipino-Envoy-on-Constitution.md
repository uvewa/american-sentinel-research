"We have spent some time with your commissioners, incidentally considering the American Constitution. Its principles impress us profoundly.

"The plan of government offered the Philippines seems, in theory, a good colonial system. But why should a nation with your Constitution seek to make a colony of a distant people who have been so long fighting against Spain to secure the same rights your Constitution gives? You fought the same battle in America when you fought against England."
