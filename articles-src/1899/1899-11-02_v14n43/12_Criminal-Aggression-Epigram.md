"CRIMINAL aggression" is shocking. "Imperialism" is too bad. "Expansion" is only tolerable. "Duty," "destiny," "the logic of events," "Providence," "be-nevolent assimilation"—these make better battle cries, especially for a Presidential campaign.—Rev. Herbert S. Bigelow.
