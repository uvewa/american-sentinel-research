With the new volume of the SENTINEL there will begin a series of articles treating of the relation of the National W. C. T. U. to the question of Sunday legislation, as fixed by the action of the late W. C. T. U. national convention touching it. And these will be articles which no person who reads this notice can afford to miss. Do not forget this, and do not forget to mention it to your W. C. T. U. and other friends.
