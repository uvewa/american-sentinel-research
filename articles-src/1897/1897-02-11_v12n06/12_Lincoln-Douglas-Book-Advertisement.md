THE extracts from the speeches of Lincoln and Douglas, presented in this paper, are taken from a volume enentitled, "Political Speeches and Debates of Lincoln and Douglas." It is a book of 555 pages and is indispensable to every one who would understand the fundamental principles of the Government and the history of the country. Price, $1.50.

To every person who reads this number of the AMERICAN SENTINEL, we say, Please see that a copy of it is put into the hands of all your neighbors, and keep a copy for yourself for reference. It is needed by everybody now, and will be needed more, later on, and you will be glad if then you have it at hand.
