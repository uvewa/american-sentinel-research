IF there were no other proof for the proposition that this is not a Christian nation, its truth would be amply demonstrated by the prevailing cruel, senseless, revolting worship of the goddess of Fashion in the form of the decoration of ladies' bonnets with the lifeless bodies of the innocent, beautiful, songsters that do so much for the farmer against injurious insects and contribute so largely to maintain what little natural beauty is still left in this dark old earth.
