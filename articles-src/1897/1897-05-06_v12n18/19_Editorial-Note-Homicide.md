People wonder and are startled at the increase of homicide in this country, and ask what can be done to check it; but in the public mind there is but a faint conception of the truth that murder belongs in the same class with all the "works of the flesh," which include many quite "respectable" sins, and that what is needed to lessen the one, is the same thing that is needed to diminish the other; namely, the grace of God in the heart.
