AT the last annual meeting of the California State Sabbath Association, a prominent clergyman from San José said in a speech that about thirty members of his church could not keep the Sabbath because there was no State Sunday law. Do professed ministers of the gospel really believe that the Lord is dependent on a State law for securing obedience to one of his requirements?
