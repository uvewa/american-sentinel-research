THE New York World sums up the situation of the governmental contest against the monopolies, by the remark that "in the campaign carried on by our patriotic legislators and Attorney-General against the Trust we have observed that although the Trusts are completely defeated in every engagement they are invariably found in possession of the battle-ground the next morning."
