It is reported that the Christian Endeavor society will next month send in a monster petition of a million and a half of names to Congress, calling for the recognition of God in the Constitution. Have our Christian Endeavor friends taken pains thoughtfully to consider the real nature of the thing which they will thus so forcibly ask the government to do?
