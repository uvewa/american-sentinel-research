THAT individual is only half awake who "wakes up" to the need of legislative measures to repress the "increasing desecration of the sabbath." If he were fully awake he would realize that the situation everywhere is far more serious than can be dealt with by any measures short of those which embody the power of God himself, manifested for the saving of the people from their sins.
