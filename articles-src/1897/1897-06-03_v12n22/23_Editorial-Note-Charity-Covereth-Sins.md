By their action in the case of the Rev. W. H. Cossum, who criticised the acceptance by the Baptist Mission Board of a bequest of $250,000 from a well known millionaire, the Board seem to have put a peculiar interpretation upon the scripture that "charity covereth a multitude of sins."
