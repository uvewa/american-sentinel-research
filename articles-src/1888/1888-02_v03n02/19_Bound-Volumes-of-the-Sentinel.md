We can furnish Volume 1, 1886, and Volume 2, 1887, of the American Sentinel, with Index, bound in strong paper covers, at 60 cents per volume post-paid. Either volume in cloth binding for $1.00 post-paid. The two volumes bound in *one,* in cloth, with gilt title, for $1.75 post-paid, or for $1.50 if taken at the Office or sent with other books by freight where we do not have to pay the 25 cents postage.[17]
