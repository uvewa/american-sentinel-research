The North Ohio Methodist Episcopal Conference lately held at Canal Dover, unanimously requested the Bishop to appoint Rev. J. P. Mills to the work of "District Secretary of the National Reform Association." The Bishop, Mallalieu, made the appointment, and shook hands with Mr. Mills, wishing him "abundant success" in his new work.
