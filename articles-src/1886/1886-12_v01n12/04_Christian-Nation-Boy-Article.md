The *Christian Nation* greatly prides itself that it was enabled lately to present an article on National Reform, which was written by a boy of fourteen years. But in this it has not much to boast of, for if the National Reform arguments generally, be judged by any real merit that they possess, or the knowledge of the principles of government which they display, they might well all be thought to be excogitated by boys of fourteen years of age.
