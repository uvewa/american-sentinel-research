## Sunday at Salem, Massachusetts.

A MEETING of the Congregational Club at Salem, Massachusetts, October 6, at which questions was discussed under the heads of "Our Town's," "Church's," and "School's," "Greatest Need," "Sunday Rest;" "Sunday Reading," were freely and ably discussed by the speakers.
