We have received a copy of the Song Friend, a monthly magazine devoted to music, and practical suggestions to musical students. It will prove a help to this class; and entertaining reading to any one at all interested in the art. It is published by S. W. Straub & Co., 243 State St., Chicago. The subscription price is $1.00.
