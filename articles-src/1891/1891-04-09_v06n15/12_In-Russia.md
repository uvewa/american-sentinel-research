**OFFICIAL.**—You cannot stay in this country, sir.

Traveler.—Then I'll leave it.

Official.—Have you a permit to leave?

Traveler.—No, sir.

Official.—Then you can not go. I give you twenty-four hours to make up your mind as to what you shall do.
