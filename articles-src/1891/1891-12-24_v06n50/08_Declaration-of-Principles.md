## NATIONAL Religious Liberty Association

### DECLARATION OF PRINCIPLES.

We believe in the religion of the Bible; and recognizing the civil government, and the Church as being ordained of God; we believe that the Church and State are separate and distinct institutions, and that neither has any right to assume the powers nor the functions of the other; and that their union constitutes the "beast" of Revelation and history.

No. 43 Bond St., New York City.

C. ELDRIDGE,
President.

C. P. BOLLMAN,
Secretary.

A. F. BALLENGER,
Corresponding Secretary.
