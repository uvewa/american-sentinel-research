Senator Carey of Wyoming, takes the place of Mr. Blair in the Senate Committee on Education and Labor, and the mantle of the chairmanship of the select Senate Committee to Establish the University of the United States falls upon Senator Proctor, as successor to Ex-Senator Edmunds.
