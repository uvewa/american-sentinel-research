THE CITY COUNCIL of Utica, New York, recently suspended the ordinance forbidding Sunday baseball, and again at a subsequent meeting reconsidered their action. As reported, the aldermen seem to have been mainly influenced in this course by the religious feeling and sentiment of fellow-townmen and church members, instead of a considerate investigation of the civil principles involved.
