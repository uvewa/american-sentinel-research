A LOCAL organization to create sentiment favoring Sunday legislation was formed at Du Quoin, Illinois, on May 10. The pastor of the Christian Church was elected president, and all the other pastors of the city, vice-presidents. Why not elect a few common citizens if the matter is wholly civil?
