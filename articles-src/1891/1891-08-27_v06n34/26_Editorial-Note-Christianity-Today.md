THE Christianity of to-day is no more the Christianity of the Bible, than was the Christianity of the third and fourth centuries. Yet out of those centuries came the worst system of iniquity which ever cursed the world. But this system recognized God in the government, and was used for the conservation of what was then considered vital to Christianity.
