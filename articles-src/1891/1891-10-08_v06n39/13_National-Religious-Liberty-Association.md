# NATIONAL Religious Liberty Association.

[page 310]

THERE Bureau of the National Religious Liberty Association is composed of competent lecturers on various parts of the General Subjects that come and showing matters upon the general subject of religious liberty and the relation of Church and State, at every opportunity. For particulars address Allen Moon, the Secretary of the Bureau, 35 College Avenue, Battle Creek, Michigan.
