Petitions for a national Sunday law are still flowing into Congress. True, the stream is not nearly so large as it was a year and a half ago, when Mr. Crafts was manipulating the springs that fed it; but these petitions, nevertheless, show a dangerous tendency in the direction of religious legislation.
