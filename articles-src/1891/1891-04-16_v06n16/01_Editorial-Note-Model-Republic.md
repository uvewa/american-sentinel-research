TAKING everything into consideration we are justified in saying that this is the best Government in existence, and could the principles of the Constitution and the true spirit of equality, freedom, and liberty, that Lincoln spoke of in the dedication of the monument at Gettysburg, be carried out, it would be indeed the model Republic.
