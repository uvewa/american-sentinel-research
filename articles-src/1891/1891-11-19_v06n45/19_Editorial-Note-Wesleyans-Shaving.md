WHILE the American Sabbath Union has been attempting to close barber-shops on Sunday, the Wesleyans, in Conference at Grand Rapids, Mich., have solved the whole problem by deciding that the "mutilation of Nature's handiwork by trimming the whiskers is against the law of God," and instructing the revision committee to "report a resolution against shaving."
