At the Congress of the Protestant Episcopal Church, just held in Washington, D. C., Ex-Senator Edmunds said, "the Church in its true mission, and in the exercise of its true functions is independent of the State, as the State in its true functions is, and always ought to be, separate from, and independent of, any church."
