The Waterloo, (Wisconsin) Republicans of July 4, contains a list of fifty-three business firms who have agreed together to close their stores on Sunday. It is the inalienable right of these gentlemen to close at any time they choose, and if every business man in the United States would voluntarily close his place of business on Sunday, that it is as submissive of all the correct principles of existing civil to compel any by statute to do on Sunday, or any other day.
