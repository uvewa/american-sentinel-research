FIRST PHILADELPHIAN: "Well, a number of the neighbors have combined and sent to Wiggins a polite note asking him to leave town."

SECOND PHILADELPHIAN: "What's the trouble? Is Wiggins a Sabbath breaker?"

FIRST PHILADELPHIAN: "No; but he is understood to have a barrel of cider in his cellar, and we are informed that the said cider worked on Sunday."—America.
