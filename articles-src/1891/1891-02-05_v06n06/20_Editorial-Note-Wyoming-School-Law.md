THE lower branch of the Wyoming Legislature recently passed the following as part of the school law of that State:— "All religious exercises, such as reading the Bible, praying, etc., are hereby prohibited in the public schools or any educational institution in the State of Wyoming, which is supported by and is under control of the State, during the sessions of such school or educational institution. The teacher or principal of any school or educational institution violating the provisions of this section shall be immediately removed from his or her position, by the Board of school trustees."
