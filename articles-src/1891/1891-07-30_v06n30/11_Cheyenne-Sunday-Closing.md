The Mayor of Cheyenne, Wyoming, has notified the saloon keepers of that place that hereafter the Sunday-closing law will be rigidly enforced against them. Why not enforce the law impartially? If the saloons are properly open during the remainder of the week, why especially discriminate against them on the first day?
