THE MAN WHO SITS IN JUDGMENT ON ANOTHER IN THE MATTER OF HIS RELIGION, AND THEN ANTIMENTS TO PUNISH HIM FOR WHAT HE IS PLEASED TO TERM "HERSEY," SIMPLY USURPS A PREROGATIVE OF GOD.
