REV. DR. WILLIAM J. R. TAYLOR, Corresponding Secretary of the American Sabbath Union, has removed his office from New York, to Washington, D. C. The "Pearl of Days" says that "as the able representative of the national society for the preservation of the Christian Sabbath, the day of rest and worship, he has already received a cordial welcome from many influential residents of Washington."
