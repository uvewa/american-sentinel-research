FEBRUARY 9, the following resolution was offered in the United States Senate and referred to the Committee on Foreign Relations:â€”

> Resolved, That the Senate of the United States has heard with great concern the statements in the newspapers in regard to the alleged persecutions of the Jews in Russia, and also in reference to the cruel treatment of State prisoners in Siberia, and other places of imprisonment in the dominions of the Czar.

> And the President of the United States is hereby requested to appeal, on the ground of humanity, to the Emperor of Russia to take measures to inquire into these alleged wrongs and cruelties to the subjects of Russia, and to place them in a condition of freedom and equal right.
