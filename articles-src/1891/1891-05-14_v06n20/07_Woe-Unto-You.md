THERE are a very few preachers and thinkers in our country who have not the natural ability, or training, to define the difference between twelve-[157]dler and teetds-ism. Our pulpit and platform ability can evolve a discourse from an adverb, a conjunction, a preposition, can divide into legistlational sections the most abstruse species of capillary matter that was ever found in a second growth.
