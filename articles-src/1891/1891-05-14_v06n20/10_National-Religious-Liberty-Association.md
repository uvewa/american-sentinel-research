## NATIONAL RELIGIOUS LIBERTY ASSOCIATION.

### DECLARATION OF PRINCIPLES.

We believe in the religion of the Bible as taught by Jesus Christ.

We believe in temperance, and regard the liquor traffic as a curse to society.

We believe in supporting the civil government, and in obeying what is of any civil government in legislating to have.

We believe, without exception, that the civil rights, which are equal for all people, should be granted to all.

We deny to believe it be our duty to not say out to every bill or which it may set all to purpose or law unjust and their civil religion.

That we warn the people of our community against the insidious attacks of the AMERICAN SENTINEL upon Sabbath-day institutions, and also of those who aim to defeat or extinguish legislation designed to perpetuate to all our people the power valued of the Christian liberty.
