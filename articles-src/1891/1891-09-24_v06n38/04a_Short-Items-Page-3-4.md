Such sentiments are disloyal to the spirit of a free government, and to the gospel of Christ, it matters not who utters them.

---

If these words had been written concerning the Sunday-law crusade, the facts could not have been better described. It is proposed by Congress to compel all political and personal letters until a Sunday law is secured; not because the law makers are convinced that such a measure is wise or just, but in order to silence the clamor of those in the demanding.

---

No one can fail to see what will be the result of having all our laws bent and distorted to "Christianize." Those who do not conform to the ideas of those who Christianize in them will be kindly told that they can go to Greenland, or the South Sea Islands, and, with the comforting assurance of having a pair of mittens or a palm-leaf fan. Come and then if they die, or if they see fit to return here, "abide the consequences." Again we ask, in this the gospel?

A. T. COLCORD.
