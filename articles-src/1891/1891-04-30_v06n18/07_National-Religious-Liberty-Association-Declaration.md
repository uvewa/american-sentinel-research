## NATIONAL RELIGIOUS LIBERTY ASSOCIATION.

*"The religious liberty sought by Jesus Christ, and purchased by the sacrifice upon the cross, guarantees to every man the right to worship God in accordance with the dictates of his own conscience."—Bancroft.*

*"In proportion as the church and the ministers lost the confidence of the people, good morals declined."—D'Aubigne.*

*"The civil magistrate has no jurisdiction in the realm of faith."—Bishop Hoadley.*

*"We believe in the live to say that in our every land, and that it shall to every citizen the free exercise of his religion, according to the dictates of his own conscience, without hindrance or persecution of both religious and civil liberty."*

*"We believe to be in our duty in our every land, and every need."*

---

C. ELDRIDGE, *President.*

A. F. BALLENGER,

D. E. R. WAMSETTER, CLARA E. BOWERS,

*A. A. Colcord, Secretary.*
