## Summary

This issue was particularly challenging due to:
1. Variable scan quality across pages
2. Very small type size in many sections
3. Tight column spacing leading to potential character confusion
4. Some areas with ink bleed or fading

The major articles ("American Principles," "On Moral Legislation," "The Hypocrisy of the Sunday-Law Movement") are captured in their essential arguments and structure, though individual phrases throughout have LOW to MEDIUM confidence. A physical copy or higher-resolution scan would be needed to verify many specific readings.
