THE Ohio Senate has passed the Schulte bill which provides that the question of closing the saloons of Cincinnati on Sunday, shall be submitted to popular vote. By such measures as this thousands will be deceived into giving adherence to Sunday laws under the delusion that they are voting for temperance and civil order.
