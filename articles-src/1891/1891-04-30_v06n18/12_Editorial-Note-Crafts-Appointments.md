REV. W. F. CRAFTS has published his appointments for the next three months as follows: May 1, Cresco, Iowa; May 4, Winchester, Iowa; May 6, Waverly, Iowa; May 10, Marshalltown, Iowa; June 22, etc., Fremont Chautauqua, Neb.; July 21, etc., Winona Chautauqua, Wis.; August 1, 2, Lakeside Chautauqua, Ohio; September, Western Pennsylvania.
