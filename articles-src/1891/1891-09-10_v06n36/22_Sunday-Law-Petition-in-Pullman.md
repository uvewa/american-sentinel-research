A PETITION has just been circulated in Pullman, Wash., to have the old Washington Territory Sunday law enforced. When it was presented to the Council, a lawyer who had been reading some good literature on the subject of Sunday laws, gave some arguments against such legislation that kept the authorities from acting in the matter.
