ON A RECENT SUNDAY EVENING, Sunday-law meetings were held in all the churches in Spokane, Wash. Full houses are reported and considerable interest is evidently felt in the matter. As usual in church circles the arguments for a religious day were disguised by a veneer of "civil Sabbath" arguments.
