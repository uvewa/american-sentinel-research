ANOTHER number of that excellent series of books—the "Young People's Library"—has been issued. It is entitled, "John of Wycliffe," and is one of the best of the series. The story of that eminent Reformer is told in that direct and interesting manner so natural to the author, Mrs. Emma H. Adams. It is an excellent book. Price in cloth embossed in jet and gold, 60 cents; in paper, 25 cents. Pacific Press, Oakland, Cal., and 43 Bond St., New York City.
