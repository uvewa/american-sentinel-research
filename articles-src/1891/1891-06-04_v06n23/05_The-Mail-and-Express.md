## The *Mail and Express*.

ROME is more persecuting Methodists as well as some of the sufferers of the poor people who, for their conscience have proceeded with the other church, have been banished, imprisoned, robbed and trampled. How long will the civilized Christian nations allow this sort of thing to go on without a sharp and effectual protest? Last Salisbury's speech, shows that he is almost ready, if backed by public opinion, to call the Romans here to account. The barbarism continually practiced in Rome, are an affront to the civilized world, and deserves the severest condemnation.

The *Mail and Express* has not, however, so far as we are aware, entered any protest against the barbarous practices in our own country upon Seventh-day Christians, merely for following upon Sunday their ordinary vocations, after having conscientiously observed the sacred day. Will our contemporary whose destinies are presided over by the President of the American Sabbath Union, rise and explain why religious persecution is not in Russia than in the United States? The principle is certainly the same, the Russians simply carry it farther than the religious bigots in this country have yet been able to do.
