Many people, in all parts of the country, are deeply interested in the investigation of the Sabbath question. To aid such we make the following combination offer to both old and new subscribers to this paper. For $1.50 we will send the American Sentinel one year, and the "History of the Sabbath and First Day of the Week," post-paid, to any address. This book contains a mine of information. Every text of Scripture concerning the Sabbath is commented on at length. Should be read by everybody. 528 pages. Address, Pacific Press, Publishers, Oakland, Cal. [17]
