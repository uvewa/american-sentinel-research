ROBERT T. NASH, a Seventh-day Adventist, was on the 10th inst. convicted at Aberdeen, Miss., of "violating the sabbath" and was sentenced to pay fine and costs amounting to $27.80, or in default to spend nearly eight months in the chain-gang. His offense was hoeing corn on Sunday.
