An exchange calls attention to the attitude of the Democratic nominee for President, Mr. W. J. Bryan, toward Sunday enforcement, by pointing out that as a congressman in July, 1892, he voted "No" on the question of making the congressional appropriation for the World's Fair conditional upon Sunday closing.
