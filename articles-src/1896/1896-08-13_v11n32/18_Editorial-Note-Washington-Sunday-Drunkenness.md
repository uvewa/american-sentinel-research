It is asserted by one who has been an observer of the facts, that in Washington, D. C., there are more men arraigned in the courts on Monday mornings for drunkenness than in any other three days of the week. And yet Washington is a city which has Sunday prohibition. This is the way Sunday closing works in the interests of temperance.
