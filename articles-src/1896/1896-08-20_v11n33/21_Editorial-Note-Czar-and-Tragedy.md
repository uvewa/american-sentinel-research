THE Czar, so it is said, has solemnly proclaimed that the great tragedy which attended his coronation ceremonies, when several thousands of his subjects were trampled to death on the plain before Moscow, was an "act of God." But, fearful that the people consider it otherwise, he is taking extraordinary precautions against assassination.
