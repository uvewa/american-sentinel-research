WE are informed that Elder Garrett, the Adventist minister, threatened by the attorney-general with indictment for giving to jurymen copies of the AMERICAN SENTINEL, was not indicted. Whether Attorney-General Lewis' good sense came to the rescue or the grand jury revolted, we are not informed.
