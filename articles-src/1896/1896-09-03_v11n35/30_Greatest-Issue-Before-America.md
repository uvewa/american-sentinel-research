THE greatest issue before the American people to-day is not that of free silver, but that of free speech and free action in fulfilling the dictates of conscience. The foremost champion of liberty in this contest with religious despotism is the AMERICAN SENTINEL. What are you doing, reader, to enlarge its field of usefulness?
