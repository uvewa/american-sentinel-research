NEW ORLEANS, La., Ravenswood and Astoria, N. Y., Waterbury, Conn., Atlantic City, N. J., Oshkosh, Wis., and Brazil, Ind., are a few of the places where the gospel of force is being invoked by clergymen and others in the interests of Sunday observance.
