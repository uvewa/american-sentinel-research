The worst form of anarchy is that which has the sanction of "law." The worst form of evil is that which wears a religious guise. The devil is never so dangerous as when he appears as an angel of light.

The thing most essential to the stability and perpetuity of Republican government, is not a blind obedience by the people to man-made legislation, but a popular allegiance to right and justice against all forms of opposition.
