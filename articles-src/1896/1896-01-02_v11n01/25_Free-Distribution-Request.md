R. T. Woodward, 66 Emerald St., Boston, Mass., desires unsolled copies of the AMERICAN SENTINEL for free distribution in that city. Postage should be fully prepaid. Four copies can be sent for one cent. The rate on such matter is one cent for four ounces or fraction thereof. Nine copies properly wrapped would cost but two cents.
