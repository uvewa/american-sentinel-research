It was hoped that the great "Christian" powers of the world would intervene to stop the fearful butchery of helpless and innocent people by the Turks in Armenia; but now, behold, these "Christian" nations are stripping for a fight among themselves, over a dispute arising from greed; and the Turk is left to continue his bloody work unchecked.
