THE Ministerial Association, composed of many good clergymen, has undertaken to wrestle with the "ideal newspaper." Softly, dear brethren. The ideal newspaper will come in time with the ideal sermon. In the meantime why not render unto Cæsar the things that are Cæsar's, and unto God the things that are God's?—New York Herald.
