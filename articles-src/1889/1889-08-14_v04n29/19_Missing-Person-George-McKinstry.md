We are requested to state that on or about the 7th of March last, George D. McKinstry, a young man eighteen years of age, left Healdsburg, this State, to return to his home at Binghampton, Cal., and has not been heard of since. No cause can be assigned for his sudden disappearance and prolonged absence. Any information as to the young man's whereabouts, either now or at any time since his disappearance, will be thankfully received by his father, H. H. McKinstry, Binghampton, Cal. [233]
