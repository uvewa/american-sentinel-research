The National Reform Association will celebrate its quarter centennial with a National Conference in connection with its annual meeting in Pittsburg, April 23-25. Eight sessions will be held. All Woman's Christian Temperance Unions, Sabbath Associations and Committees, Young Men's Christian Associations, etc., are invited to send delegates.
