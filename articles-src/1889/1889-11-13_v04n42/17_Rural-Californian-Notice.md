*The Rural Californian,* "a journal for the suburban and country homes," published by Heintz & Lipe, Los Angeles, Cal., is a worthy magazine of more than usual value. No one who contemplates settling in Southern California can do better than to subscribe for and read this journal. It will acquaint you with the State in advance of your coming. It can be had six months for seventy-five cents; single number, fifteen cents.
