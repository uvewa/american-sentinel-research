August 3 to 5, Dr. W. F. Crafts spent in San Francisco and Oakland. He spoke at Howard St. M. E. Church at 11 a. m., and at United Presbyterian Church at 8 p. m., Sunday; and to the San Francisco pastors at the Y. M. C. A. Hall, Monday, at 3 p. m., and at First Congregational Church, Oakland, Monday evening. We shall notice his speeches in future issues of the Sentinel.
