# Editorial Notes

THE Mountain State Gavel, of New Martinsville, W. Va., reprints in its February issue our article of January 17 entitled "An American Citizen Defends his Faith and his Rights." We appreciate these efforts of our exchanges to serve the cause of truth and religious liberty.
