# THE "CIVIL SABBATH" FRAUD.

ADULTERY, theft, and murder are crimes on all days and in all places. Labor, business, and recreation are neither crimes nor misdemeanors, but rather necessities and fundamental virtues. Sunday laws declare that these virtues become civil crimes and misdemeanors if performed on Sunday, and the religious leaders declare them to be sins.
