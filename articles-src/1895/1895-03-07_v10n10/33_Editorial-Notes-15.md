# Editorial Notes

WE have on hand a good supply of back numbers of the SENTINEL of recent dates, among which are the issues of January 24 and 31, February 7, 14 and 21. All these numbers are valuable to missionary workers for free distribution, and will be furnished in assorted lots at the rate of one dollar per hundred. Send in your orders for any small quantity at this rate. $8 per thousand.
