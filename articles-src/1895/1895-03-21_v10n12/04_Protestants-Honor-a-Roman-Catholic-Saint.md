## PROTESTANTS HONOR A ROMAN CATHOLIC SAINT.

BRADDOCK, Pa., March 9.—The Ministerial Association of this city, composed of Catholic and Protestant clergy of all denominations, has decided to ring all church bells in the city on St. Patrick's Day.—The World, March 10.
