# QUOTATIONS

THERE are many who do not seem to be sensible that all violence in religion is irreligious, and that, whoever is wrong, the persecutor cannot be right.—Thomas Clarke.

EQUAL liberty was originally the portion and is still the birthright of all men.—B. Franklin.
