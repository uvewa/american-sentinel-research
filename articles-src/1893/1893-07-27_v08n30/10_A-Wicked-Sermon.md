In order to understand fully the wickedness of this sermon, it is important to know that the building that was burned was not open on Sunday. The dishonesty of the sermon lay in stating that the destruction of the burned building was on the part of the contractor who built it rather than by its destruction. The firemen who lost their lives had nothing to do with the opening of the Fair; and the contortions of the most respectable clergy who applauded the decision of the directory to close the gates on Sunday. There was simply no connection between the fire and Sunday opening. The World's Fair on a Sunday is closed.
