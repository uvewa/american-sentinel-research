Bachelder, of the First Presbyterian Church (Colgrove Park, Wis.), was the subject of charges of heresy brought by a number of elders to such a pitch that they have determined the Presbytery also opposed a demand to have him brought before the synod. Several elders, however, who support the pastor,

stand in the way and may prevent any action. Mr. Bachelder's preaching is wholly a protest against all classes. He is opposed to the opening of the Fair Sunday, and said that the clergymen petitioning to provide a proviso for the work of Christians in Chicago, he declared that it be opened on Sunday is unconstitutional and destructive.

Whatever else may be the result of the agitation of this Sunday question in connection with the closing of the Fair, it is certain to lead many to a correct understanding of the proper relation of Church and State.
