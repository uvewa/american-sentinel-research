IT is stated that the persecutions to be added to the Russian province have been expelled from the Roods-Roumelia and various other places. Five hundred families have been expelled; that those expelled and engaged in trade and some pawned to their entire ruin and had were barely to education. But what rights have Adventists to find fault with the Czar? He is simply trying to suit and strengthen his empire, and in this matter of religion the Czar has even less substantial grounds.
