THAT SMITH, WALL, Missionary Secretary, sent us a large number of copies of the *Sentinel*, *Signs*, *Review*, *Youth's Instructor* and *Little Friend* in this way. "Every person having cancelled copies of these or any other of our periodicals and papers, if they do well to send them, carriage paid." T. E. Smith, Battle Creek, Mich.
