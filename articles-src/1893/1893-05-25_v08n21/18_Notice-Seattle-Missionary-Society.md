The Seattle, Wash., Missionary Society, can use a large number of copies of the SENTINEL, Signs, Review, Youth's Instructor and Little Friend in the ship work. Persons having unsoiled copies of these papers for which they have no use, will do well to send them, carriage paid, to E. T. Cornell, Seattle, Wash.
