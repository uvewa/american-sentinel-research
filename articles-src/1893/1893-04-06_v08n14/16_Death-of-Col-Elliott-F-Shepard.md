COL. ELLIOTT F. SHEPARD, President, of the American Sabbath Union, and editor of the Mail and Express, died at his home, in this city, on the afternoon of Friday, March 24. His death was entirely unexpected, resulting from the administration of other preparatory to performing the operation of lithotomy.
