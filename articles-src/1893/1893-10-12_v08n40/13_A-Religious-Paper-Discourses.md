A RELIGIOUS paper discourses as follows to the public good: "Those who oppose Sunday, by which it means those who teach that Sunday is not the Sabbath and the seventh day is the Sabbath. Of course "treason to the public good" ought to be punished, or, at least restrained. That the logic of any religious institution be invented in civil matters.
