Now that the United States Court has decided not to set the example, the courts all seem more kindly disposed toward Sunday opening of the Fair, and not the first that the Washington & Rowan bill, which sought an injunction to close the great show on Sunday, was thrown out of course. Judge Jenkins granted the prayer for closing.
