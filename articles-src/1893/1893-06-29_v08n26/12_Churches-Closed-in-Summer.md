IN the large cities a great many churches are closed during the first summer months. If, of the preachers succeed in closing the parks also and stopping the Sunday short of trains, there will still be the people who are not, what the problem will be as to how to stop the movement in the moonlight? It matters not to the Sunday preachers, if only their darling is up.
