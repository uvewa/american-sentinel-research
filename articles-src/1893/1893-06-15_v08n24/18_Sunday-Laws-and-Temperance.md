IT SEEMS Sunday laws were urged in the interests of temperance. The *Sentinel* said all the time that the real object was to compel temperance legislation but to teach religion. Now the facts demonstrate the truth of our contention. A great part of the demand for Sunday closing was very genuine in its results, but the demand that is to be sold on the grounds; now the people are sold also, there, nothing but all boycotting the Fair is that reason fact for Sunday opening; and not only so, but the *Political Democrat*, a National Reform paper published at Allegheny, Pa., a journal that object has recurred to all the churches.

Petitions were sent to Congress establishing that petition, the signatures the population of various States; lobbyists were in Washington in masses, and congressmen, at every conference given were urged upon them that this was not a measure of religion.
