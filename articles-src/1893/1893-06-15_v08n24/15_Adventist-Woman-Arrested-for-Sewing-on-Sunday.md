A WOMAN, a member of the Seventh-day Adventist Church, at Ford's Store, Queen Anne's County, Md., has been arrested for sewing on Sunday. Her trial was set for the 14th inst. The woman's own brother is the complaining witness.

At the same time two male members of the same church were arrested for hauling some goods from the wharf on Sunday. In these cases the complaining witness was also the brother of one of the arrested men.
