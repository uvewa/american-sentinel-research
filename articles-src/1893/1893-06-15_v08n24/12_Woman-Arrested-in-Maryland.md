A WOMAN, a member of the Seventh-day Adventist Church, at Forks Store, Anne's Arundel County, Md., has been arrested for sewing on Sunday. Her trial was set for the 11th inst. Two members own brother in the complaining witness. At the same time two male members of the same church were arrested for hauling some goods from the wharf on Sunday, making it three from the same congregation, and she was the brother of one of the witnesses also.
