IT is stated that the Pope has instructed Monsignor Satolli to express to Cardinal Gibbons his thanks for the discourses recently delivered by the cardinal in favor of the restoration of the temporal power of the Pope, and to inform his eminence that the great infallible is entirely satisfied with the language and argument of the cardinal in the discourses.
