IT is announced that the Department of Sunday Rest of the World's Fair Congress Auxiliary will hold meetings on September 25-28, at Chicago. The subjects discussed will be included in the following divisions: the Physiological, the Economical and National, the Reformatory and the Social and Moral, and the Religious Relations of the Weekly Rest Day.
