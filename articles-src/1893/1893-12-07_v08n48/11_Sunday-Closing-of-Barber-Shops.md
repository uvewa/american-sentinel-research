IN Kansas City, Mo., Denver, Col., and Detroit, Mich., the Sunday closing of barber shops is occupying the attention of the Sunday law advocates. Success is attending their efforts to compel the unwillingly hirsute to wear upon their faces the evidence of their Sunday observance.
