IN Kansas City, Mo., Dayton, Col., and Detroit, Mich., the Sunday closing of barber shops is occupying the attention of the Sunday law advocates. Strenuous but vain efforts to compel the unwilling to wrest upon those faces the evidence of their Sunday observance.
