It is to be presumed that those Sunday-"Christians" are worse than their fellows in other States; hence we must conclude from the facts given in the *Statesman* that one-third of all the petitioners for Sunday-closing were moved to petition by policy and not by principle; for when opportunity offered to save a gallery man by visiting the Fair on a certain day they did not scruple to travel on Sunday to do it. After closing the Fair to others on Sunday, they themselves traveled on the day in order to attend the saloon on Monday!
