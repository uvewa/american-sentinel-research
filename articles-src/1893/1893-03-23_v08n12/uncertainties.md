# Uncertainties — American Sentinel, March 23, 1893 (Vol. 8, No. 12)

## General Notes

This issue presented significant OCR challenges due to image resolution and the dense column layout of the newspaper. Many passages throughout the issue were difficult to read with full confidence. The transcriptions represent best-effort readings, but numerous passages may contain errors due to the difficulty of reading the source images.

  - **Obstruction:** Dense small text, difficult to read clearly

  - **Confidence:** LOW — The opening words of this paragraph are very difficult to read; multiple words in this paragraph may be incorrect
