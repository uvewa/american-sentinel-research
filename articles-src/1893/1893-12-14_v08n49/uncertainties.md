# Uncertainties - American Sentinel, Volume 8, Number 49 (December 14, 1893)

## General Note

The source images for this issue are at a resolution that makes many words and phrases difficult to read with full confidence. The text is small and some areas are slightly blurred. Many passages throughout this issue required best-effort interpretation. Below are the most significant areas of concern.

## Page 1 - Front Page Editorial ("The Situation To-day")

- **Confidence:** MEDIUM throughout
- The front page editorial paragraphs are readable but some phrases in the third column are uncertain, particularly in the final paragraph about "Protestant satellite to the Papacy."
- The opening paragraph may contain errors in the phrase "that the power of the Church or State to compel the observance of the State" - the second "State" may be another word.
