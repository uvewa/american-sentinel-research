IT is only paying salutation to the World's Fair that committees of Congress seems to think, for in the issue of July 3, it says, "Only 17,574 descendants," on last Sunday; yet it is presently told of 16,385 free admissions to the grounds on the same date, making the total number of visitors 34,138, including, evidently, from the details given, at least one reporter for the "leading evening paper," the only great anti-religion religious daily, whose "copy" consist in advertising Piper Henderson Sec, and demanding Sunday laws for other people.
