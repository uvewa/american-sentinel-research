The Bible commands me to keep the Sabbath holy. Nowhere does the Bible command me to keep Sunday. I recognize the authority of the Bible. The Seventh-day Adventist must be, and is, I say, a Saturday, i. e., Saturday; for we know Sunday is not the Sabbath. I am a poor and ignorant man, and I do not consider myself competent to discuss this question. I am not a rich man, but I will give any Sabbath observance, besides what be to keep Sunday, that they cannot refute.

We are praying for the Fair because it is open on Sunday, and for more biblical observance.
