# Abiding Sabbath and Lord's Day.

A pointed review of the $100 and $1,500 prize essays of the "Abiding Sabbath," so called. Those desiring some $1,000 reasons for keeping the first day of the week, will find them here. 178 pages, 90 cents. Pacific Press, 43 Bond St., New York City.
