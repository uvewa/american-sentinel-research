On Monday, Oct. 16, sixty-three tailors, of this city, were arraigned before Justice Koch for violation of the Sunday law in carrying on their ordinary occupation on Sunday. They were held for examination on Nov. 16, and gave bail for their appearance in the sum of one hundred dollars each.
