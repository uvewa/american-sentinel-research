THE BOYCOTT.

There comes the remedies to petition that the Fair be not opened on Sundays by closing the gates on that day; and then they "engaged" to "pull folds for advising a boycott" of all Christians and law-abiding citizens of the Pacific Coast the employment of those who do not; let the Expositor, or in other words, not to patronize the Fair if opened on Sunday. The same spirit is at the bottom of all Sunday laws.
