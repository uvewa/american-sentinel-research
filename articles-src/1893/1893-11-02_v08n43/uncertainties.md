# Uncertainties — American Sentinel, November 2, 1893, Volume 8, Number 43

## General Notes

This issue presented significant OCR challenges due to:
- Small, dense print typical of 1893 newspapers
- Three-column layout with very small type
- Some areas with reduced contrast
- Several long articles spanning multiple columns and pages

## Specific Uncertainties

- **Second pass completed:** Yes
- **Confidence:** MEDIUM — The general arguments and flow are captured, but some specific phrasings in the detailed commentary sections may have minor word-level inaccuracies due to the very small type size
