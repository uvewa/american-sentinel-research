SPEAKERS of the World's Congress of Religions, to be held in Chicago September 11-27, the New York *Independent* says: "The discussions will be friendly, not controversies." But to the Council, has sent word first. Delivered by almost Rev. A. P. Fecken, D.D., Archbishop of Chicago, and to be the opening day as delivered by Right Rev. Mgr. Gruel, David Bishop of Rockwell; Sargent, and His Eminence Cardinal Moran, Archbishop of Sydney, Australia. There are ten Jewish speakers who will take part.
