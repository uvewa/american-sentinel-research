# Uncertainties — American Sentinel, June 8, 1893 (Volume 8, Number 23)

## General Notes

This issue consists of B&W scanned images at moderate resolution. The text is set in small type across three columns per page, which makes certain words and passages difficult to read with certainty, particularly in the long serialized article "The Image to the Beast" spanning pages 1-3.

## Specific Uncertainties

### Article 03 - "That a Holy Day Rests Only"
- **Location:** Page 1, left column, third editorial note
- **Second pass completed:** Yes
- **Issue:** The text of this short editorial note is partially obscured and the logic is difficult to follow. The transcription may contain errors in sentence flow.
- **Confidence:** LOW
- **Reason:** Small type and B&W scan makes individual words hard to distinguish

### Article 05 - "The Image to the Beast"
- **Location:** Pages 1-3, spanning multiple columns
- **Second pass completed:** Yes

### Overall Assessment
This issue's B&W scans are of moderate quality. The main article "The Image to the Beast" is particularly challenging due to its length and the extensive quotation of Congressional Record proceedings.
