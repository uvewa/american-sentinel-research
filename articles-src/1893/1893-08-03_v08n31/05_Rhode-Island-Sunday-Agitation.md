In the little State of Rhode Island there is agitation for a Sunday law, which State which of all the States should be the very last to entertain such a proposal, for its founder Roger Williams, who fled from the persecution of the Massachusetts theocracy in the middle of winter to establish in the wilds of America a State in which religious freedom might be established by, and Church and State forever separated might be kept forever separate.
