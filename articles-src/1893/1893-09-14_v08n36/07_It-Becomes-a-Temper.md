EVERY persecution, every cruelty, every wrong, is promoted because it is said that even thought it was the humanitarian's real purpose. The mischief that this idea is pregnant to individuals, and especially all religious errors. The mischief this idea is pregnant to individuals, becomes under all other considerations, it is enough to suggest even its most obvious tendencies which are, the increase of hypocrisy, and the increase of tyranny; by allowing to happen the inevitable result of constituting any description of penalty upon the profession of particular opinions.
