THERE is no contest in the religion itself of the gospel of Jesus Christ. The invitation, the entreaty, is, Come. The offer of the ambassadors of Christ is a personal one. Jesus says, "Whosoever will, let him come." Isaiah pleads: "Turn ye, oh, turn ye, for why will ye die?"
