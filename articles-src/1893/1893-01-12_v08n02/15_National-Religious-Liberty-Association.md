DECLARATION OF PRINCIPLES.

We believe in the religion of the Bible and the right and absurd as the privilege, of every man to worship God according to the dictates of his own conscience.

*It is also believed to be our duty in so every lawful way which may be proper to prevent any of our citizens from maintaining the same rights and liberties.*

R. E. FOSTER, *President.*
W. H. McKEE, *Secretary.*
