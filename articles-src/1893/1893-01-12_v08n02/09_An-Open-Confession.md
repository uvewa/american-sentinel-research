To repeal the religious legislation pertaining to the World's Columbian Exposition.

It is very evident that "Congress shall make no law respecting an establishment of religion, or prohibiting the free exercise thereof."

*Resolved by the Senate and House of Representatives of the United States of America in Congress assembled*, (two-thirds of each Congress assembled) one hundred and ninety-two, making the observance of Saturday or Sunday of the discovery of America by Christopher Columbus.
