JANUARY 10, Hon. John Wads, of Fergus, Minn., member of the lower House of the legislature, introduced in the lower House of the legislature, a bill for the repeal of sections, Sections 6515, 277, and 6 of the Minnesota Sunday law.

The occasion for the introduction and the provisions of this bill were as follows: On July 10, 1892, one of the citizens of the Minnesota Sunday law had committed an offense against the said Sunday law in the town of Fergus, which is in the county and district represented by Mr. Wads, a member of the place came to him after his arrest.
