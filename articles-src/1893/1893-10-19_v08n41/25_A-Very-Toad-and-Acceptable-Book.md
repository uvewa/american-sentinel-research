A VERY neat and acceptable book, by *Christian Citizenship*, by W. A. Colcord, has reached us and will be reviewed. Mr. White, has reached the editorial department for a more adequate description. *True freemen are wrought in the great principles of the Church and State.*

The spirit of this book from beginning to end, is that it should be taught to be read, in its principles, to be brought to the world, to be taught to all, be taught to venerate everything connected with the Christian Church, and to worship God.
